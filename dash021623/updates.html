Along with adjusting the locations of items, it is common for Super Metroid randomizers to modify the original source code of the game to improve the gameplay experience. Below is a breakdown of all modifications made by the DASH randomizer.
Item Utility
Changes
  • Bombs opens Wrecked Ship Reserve location
  • Spazer opens Waterway and Botwoon hallway
  • Samus begins with Starter Charge Beam that does the same damage as uncharged shots
  • Pseudo Screws and Special Beam Attacks do full damage with Starter Charge Beam
Suit
Changes
  • Gravity Suit provides 50% heat damage reduction
  • Varia and Gravity each add 50% damage reduction to enemy attacks and environmental damage
  • Varia and Gravity stack to reach 75% reduction to enemy attacks and environmental damage
Soft-Lock
Prevention
  • Speed blocks at Dachora pit no longer respawn
  • Shot block added to escape from under early Super bridge
  • Bomb blocks replaced with shot blocks before Hi-Jump Boots
  • Bomb blocks replaced with shot blocks at Moat
  • Platform raised in first heated Norfair room to not require Hi-Jump Boots
  • Platforms at bottom of Red Tower adjusted to always be able to get back up
  • Doors in Construction Zone and above Red Tower are blue
  • Animation no longer shown when suits are acquired
Convenience
  • Skips Intro and Ceres Station
  • Zebes wakes when going right from Morph
  • G4 passage to Tourian opens instantly when all bosses are killed
  • Speed up Mother Brain cutscene
  • HUD shows max ammo
  • Bomb blocks replaced with shot blocks at Spazer
  • Start screen displays randomizer seed
  • Game play statistics shown in ending credits
Bug Fixes
  • Fixed heat damage speed echoes bug
  • GT Code is disabled
  • Spacetime Beam selection disabled
  • Fixed Screw Attack menu selection bug
  • Morph Ball can be placed in Hidden and Chozo locations
  • Fixes to Morph Ball & Missiles Room States
