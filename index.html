Here is my low-tech front page for the Super Metroid romhacks I've worked on, most recent first. - My goal with hack randomizers is to preserve the original behavior of the hack as much as possible STARDUST https://randorandy.github.io/stardustRando/ - With the BT-locked door, Morph and Bombs will always be in the early area. - The final Tourian E-Tank is unaltered, as well as the first two items in the escape. RECOVERY https://randorandy.github.io/recoveryRando/ - There are no softlock protections for certain items. Consider how you will get out of Wave Beam, LN, etc. ESCAPE 2, 1.9 https://randorandy.github.io/Escape2Rando/ - The Secret Spore Spawn door to backdoor Tourian takes you to G4 statues instead, meaning G4 bosses are required. - Early Supers usually means to go to the Wrecked Ship Warp Zone, then Brinstar for more progression. ERIS 2012 https://randorandy.github.io/ErisRando/ - Artifacts and their locations are shuffled into the item pool. Find 4 of them and go win! - The "101 Missile" in the ceiling is also in the randomized pool, obtainable with Ice Beam. CRYOGENESIS 1.0 https://randorandy.github.io/cryogenesis-rando/ - Added Plasma and Speed Booster to this romhack. Speed blocks will break with Speed or Meteorite. - You will need the Meteorite to logically exit the Meteorite area. Do not enter without it. SUBVERSION 1.2 * Note, buxnq has updated and maintained this since December 2022 * https://subversionrando.github.io/SubversionRando/ - Subversion has several options for logic, area rando, and other new features.
